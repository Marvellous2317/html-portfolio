The Best K-dramas & Series According to Marvellous

My top 5 K-dramas of all-time.

Celebrity

The romance between the two leads is sooo intense. The storyline is about a young girl called Seo Ari and how you unveils the dark parts of social media and the struggles of internet influencers.

Vincenzo

Also had an amazing storyline! This is about a mafia lawyer whose boss dies and is asked to recover some gold buried under an old building in Seoul. Unluckily for him, the buildi ng has also caught the eye of some dangerous people in the government. The drama shows how navigates this with the help of the female lead a lawyer who had some dealing with the corrrupt company to be brought down.

Happiness

This drama made me soooo happy. It showed me that love can given without touching each other; basically the depth of love. The storyline is about two leads who care a lot about each other but move in together to manoeveur some military rules. Then a deadly disease struck the city and started from their complex with the female lead being the only one immune to it and being the posiible cure.

Bitch X Rich

This is about a poor highschooler who finds herself in the world of the rich after being a witness to the death of a student in the building.

Business Proposal

This drama will crack your ribs! This is about a CEO who takes over his father company but also falls in love with the nutritionist of the company. It shows how they fall in love and the struggles of belonging to different social classes in the society.
